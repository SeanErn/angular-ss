Write a letter to Laura

May 2nd, 1863
Today, I was hired as a new job at the shoe factory. We was told that we would supply the Union Army with the shoes they needs. It pays poor but better than nothing. I have managed to make myself a small house in the Grand Rapids city. Every morning I get up to walk to work, making the shoes at my new job. After my work day, I write to my diary. I go home to support my wife. Sometimes I do other things, but not much. I've heard about the horrors coming from the South. The audacity of them to think that slavery is right. I would never.
