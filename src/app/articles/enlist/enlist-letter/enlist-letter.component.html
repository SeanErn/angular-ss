June 24th, 1863
My dearest Laura, Reminiscing on old times I cannot say anything less than how much I love you. Whilst I would love to return and grow old together, I am afraid of deployment soon. I am an Union Army private now, hoping to become a higher-up soon. But, It seems I must go now, I hope this letter reaches you in good health.

-Daniel
